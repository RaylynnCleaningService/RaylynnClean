Our Story

Founded by Raymon and Lynn Gapusan, RAYLYNN CLEANING SERVICES emerged from a commitment to exceptional cleaning, meticulous attention to detail, and genuine customer satisfaction.

Our Cleaning Services

Residential Cleaning

Comprehensive cleaning services for homes of all sizes. We cover kitchens, bathrooms, bedrooms, living areas, dusting, vacuuming, mopping, and more. Customizable plans available.

Commercial Cleaning

Professional cleaning solutions for offices, retail spaces, and other commercial properties. We ensure a clean, healthy, and productive work environment for your employees and clients.

Move-In/Move-Out Cleaning

Take the stress out of moving with our specialized cleaning services. We'll deep clean the property before you move in or after you move out, ensuring it's spotless for the next occupants.

Deep Cleaning

A thorough, top-to-bottom cleaning that reaches every corner and surface. Ideal for spring cleaning, special occasions, or getting your space exceptionally clean.
